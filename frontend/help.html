Help & Documentation

How to Scan Attendance

Go to the Scan page. Select the course you are currently teaching from the dropdown. Once the camera activates, point it at a student's QR code. The system will beep and record the log automatically.

Tip: If a student forgot their QR code, use the "Manual Entry" button on the Scan page to type their username.

Managing Students

Admins can add, edit, or delete users in the Admin Panel. Professors can view class rosters by clicking on a class in the Dashboard or visiting the Reports page.

Exporting Data

To get an Excel-compatible file, go to Reports. You can filter by specific courses or dates, then click the "CSV" button to download the report.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password?
Go to Settings > Profile Settings and enter a new password.
Why is the camera not working?
Ensure your browser has permission to access the camera. Check the URL bar for a camera icon to allow access.
